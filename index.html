All fun and games.

Welcome to Monopolatté, Ottawa’s very first board game cafe! Located just outside of China Town, we are an independantly owned rad place to hang out. From Monopoly to Munchkin, we offer an unparalelled selection of games. Come on in, pull up a chair, pick a board game, and have some fun.

MENU

Grilled cheese sandwiches galore!

Come try a wide variety of flavours including brie, tomato, and bacon. All of our sandwiches are freshly made and served with a side of chips and your choice of beverage.

Beer + board games.

There is nothing quite like a good drinking game. With everything from classic Guinness to delicious Somersby cider, you can drink the night away with your friends while you destroy them in the game of Life.

There’s no such thing as junk food.

At Monopolatte we serve you a delicious side of chips with your food. Potato chips. Because we know that there’s nothing better to bring back that kid in you than junk food and an all-nighter.
